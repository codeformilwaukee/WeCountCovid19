The goal is to reach every person in the US who is potentially infected with coronavirus. This is incredibly important because it will help to inform what we need to do, and we lack the kits for this information. To the survey / A la encuesta Health of Wisconsin Respondents Testing of Persons with Symptoms These summaries pertain to survey respondents reporting a Wisconsin zip code. People responding are those who learned of the survey and were motivated to report their symptoms, and do not represent all people. We defined COVID-like symptoms using CDC website reports of typical symptoms, defined as reporting respiratory symptoms (moderate or severe cough, chest pain, or difficulty breathing) or fever of 100 or higher, and without a flu diagnosis. We defined “at risk” using CDC guidelines for testing priority, defined as aged 65+, having an underlying medical condition, or residing in a long-term care facility. Get Help Resources 2020
